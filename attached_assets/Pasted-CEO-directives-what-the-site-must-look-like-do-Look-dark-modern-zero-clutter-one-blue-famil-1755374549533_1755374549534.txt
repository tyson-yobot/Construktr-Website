CEO directives (what the site must look like & do)

Look: dark, modern, zero clutter. one blue family, one yellow CTA, mono trust icons. nothing else.

Feel: confidence + speed. every block answers: how fast will this help me quote, schedule, get paid?

Outcome: clicks on Start Free Trial, plays of the 45-sec video, demo submissions.

Offer: always-on 14-day free trial; time-boxed “1 month free for the next 250 downloads” promo with a small, classy top bar (not a takeover).

Education: the 3-step flow Quote → Schedule → Get Paid with tiny real app loops in phone frames. short bullets. proof. pricing. done.

Parity: website and app share the exact tokens (colors, type, radii), same button sizes, same card look. screenshots show only blue UI and have a tiny “Real App” bubble.

Non-negotiables (brand system)

Single source of truth
Use the exact tokens you supplied (colors, radii, shadows, button sizes). No unapproved colors, no deviations.

CSS variables live in design-system.css and are consumed by both the Next.js site and the app (Tailwind + RN theme).

Tailwind mapping: mirror the tokens and read from CSS vars so site & app stay visually locked.

Buttons (same everywhere)

Primary: yellow --clr-primary, 48–52px tall, bold 14–16px, radius 10px. Label = Start Free Trial.

Secondary: dark surface + border. Label = Book a Demo.

Ghost: transparent + border. Label = Download (utility).

No other variants allowed.

Cards & Chips

Cards: --clr-surface, 1px --clr-border, radius 20px, var(--shadow-soft).

Chips: pill 28–32px, brand-blue family only, never multicolor.
