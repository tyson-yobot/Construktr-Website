1. Product Brief – What Construktr Actually Is

Construktr is a mobile-first operating system for field service contractors (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, handyman, roofing, landscaping, etc.).

It is not a generic scheduling app. It is a full AI-assisted job management platform that lives primarily in a React Native + Expo mobile app (iOS and Android).

Key capabilities (all already implemented in the mobile app):

Jobs Management

Create, view, and manage jobs with status (pending / in progress / completed).

Store customer, location, photos, notes, and job history.

Quotes, AI Estimating & Invoicing

Photo/AR/map-based estimating (take photos, measure with AR, auto-build estimates).

AI-assisted quote pricing; line items, labor & materials.

Professional PDF quotes and invoices.

Send via email/SMS, track status (draft/sent/accepted/overdue).

Smart Scheduling & Routing

Calendar views (day/week/month) with drag-and-drop scheduling.

Route optimization using maps.

Weather-aware planning and conflict detection.

Crew assignment and recurring jobs.

Time Tracking & Geofencing

GPS-based automatic clock-in/out at job sites.

Timesheets and labor cost summaries.

Overtime calculations and exports.

CRM & Business Card OCR

Scan business cards → auto-create contacts with AI OCR.

Tag contacts, view job history, search.

Client Portal & Payments

Magic-link client portal for approving quotes, viewing invoices, and paying securely.

Stripe-powered payments; same-day deposits.

QuickBooks sync for accounting.

Marketing & Reviews

Automated review requests after job completion.

Review tracking and basic campaign tools.

Subscriptions & Plans

Multiple pricing tiers, free trial, Stripe billing.

All 28 advertised features in the mobile app are real and implemented. Your marketing copy must not invent new features or promise things the app cannot reasonably do, but you may tighten/clarify wording.
