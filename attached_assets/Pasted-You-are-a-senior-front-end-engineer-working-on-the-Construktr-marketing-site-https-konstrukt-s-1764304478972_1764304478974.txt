1. Background & Section Contrast
------------------------------------------------

Problem: The background is too light and too close to white, so cards/boxes are hard to see.

Actions:

1. Global background:
   - Adjust the page-shell background (html/body/root layout) to a slightly darker, cool silver/blue gradient so white cards stand out clearly.
   - Use something in this range (adapt syntax to the existing styling approach):

     Example:

     background: linear-gradient(
       to bottom,
       #E4E7F1 0%,
       #D7DBE6 40%,
       #C9CEDA 100%
     );

   - It must be visibly darker than white, but still light and modern.

2. Section banding:
   - Use a clean rhythm for section backgrounds:
     - Hero: can sit on the global gradient or a slightly tweaked variant.
     - Stats band: flat white or very light gray (#F9FAFB).
     - Feature grid: flat white or very light gray.
     - Trade section: flat background (white / #F9FAFB), not another strong gradient.
     - Footer: flat background with appropriate contrast.

3. Avoid gradient overload:
   - Do NOT put gradients inside cards.
   - Gradients are for page shell and (optionally) hero only.

------------------------------------------------
2. Card / Box Styling & Separation
------------------------------------------------

Problem: The boxes are visually blending into the background.

Actions:

1. Card backgrounds:
   - Set all primary cards/panels (feature cards, trade content boxes, stats cards, etc.) to:
     - background: #FFFFFF;

2. Borders:
   - Add a subtle but clearly visible border:

     border: 1px solid rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.08);

3. Shadows:
   - Add a soft shadow for depth, e.g.:

     box-shadow:
       0 10px 25px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.08),
       0 2px 8px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.04);

4. Radius & padding:
   - Normalize card radius across the site (e.g., 12–16px).
   - Normalize card padding (e.g., 20–24px) and ensure consistent spacing between cards in grids.

5. Hover/focus:
   - On desktop, add a subtle hover state for cards:
     - Slight translateY(-2px) + stronger shadow or slightly darker border.
   - Ensure focus-visible styles are present for keyboard users.

------------------------------------------------

3. Buttons:
